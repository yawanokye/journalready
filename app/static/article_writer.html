Flexible article workflow

Empirical articles use Stage 1 before results and Stage 2 after analysis. Systematic, scoping, conceptual and bibliometric articles can be developed as complete synthesis papers from verified literature or publication metadata.

Independent article mode is active. Thesis, dissertation and project inputs are disabled. Research depth is set to PhD by default. Empirical articles stop at Methods, while synthesis article types can be drafted in full.
