# ArticleReady AI Pricing Page Update

This update adds a public pricing page at `/pricing`, a navigation tab across all main pages and a homepage pricing preview.

The Article Topic Ideas entitlement is increased to up to 20 ideas for US$2.99. The frontend, schema validation and topic-idea service now accept up to 20 ideas. The structured fallback also includes additional idea templates so it can produce longer portfolios when DeepSeek is unavailable.

The pricing page lists the current packages, user entitlements and internal token allowances for cost control.
