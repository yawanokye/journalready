# Article Writer Status Message Update

The Article Writer no longer exposes the configured language model in user-facing completion messages.

## New completion messages

- Stage 1: `Stage 1 completed. The article draft is prepared through the Methods section.`
- Stage 2: `Stage 2 completed. The full article draft is ready.`
- Full article: `Article draft completed.`

Provider or model metadata remains available internally for diagnostics, but it is not shown to end users.
